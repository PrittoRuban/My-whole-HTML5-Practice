Foam Type Description
Polyurethane Foam A versatile foam that is used in a wide range of applications, including furniture, mattresses, and insulation.
Latex Foam A durable and supportive foam that is often used in mattresses and pillows.
Memory Foam A pressure-relieving foam that is often used in mattresses and pillows.
EPS Foam A lightweight and inexpensive foam that is often used in packaging and insulation.
XPE Foam A soft and flexible foam that is often used in gaskets and seals.
